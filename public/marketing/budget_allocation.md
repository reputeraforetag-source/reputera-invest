## Budget Allocation Philosophy

### Investment Principles

1. **Test Before Scale**
   - Q1: Test channels at small scale (400k SEK)
   - Q2-Q3: Scale what works (600-700k SEK)
   - Continuous optimization based on data

2. **Balanced Portfolio**
   - 40% Cold Email (proven, scalable)
   - 25% LinkedIn (brand + retargeting)
   - 20% Partnerships (strategic moat)
   - 15% Content/SEO (long-term asset)

3. **Capital Efficiency**
   - Target blended CAC: â‰¤500 SEK
   - Monthly tracking of CAC by channel
   - Reallocate budget from underperforming to high-performing channels

4. **Long-Term Building**
   - Invest in owned channels (SEO, partnerships)
   - Build assets that compound (content, integrations)
   - Not just performance marketing (brand building too)
