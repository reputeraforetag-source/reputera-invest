## Content Production Process

### Content Team Structure

**Month 1-3 (Founder-Led):**
- Founder writes outlines, key posts
- Freelance writer: 8 posts/month (2,000 SEK/post)
- SEO Specialist (Part-time): Keyword research, optimization

**Month 4+ (Scaled Team):**
- Marketing Lead: Strategy, oversight
- Content Writer (Freelance): 8-10 posts/month
- SEO Specialist (Part-time): Technical SEO, link building
- Designer (Freelance): Images, infographics (as needed)

### Content Workflow

**Week 1: Planning**
- SEO Specialist: Keyword research, content brief
- Marketing Lead: Approve topics, outlines

**Week 2: Creation**
- Writer: First draft (1,200-1,800 words)
- Designer: Featured image, in-post graphics

**Week 3: Optimization**
- SEO Specialist: On-page optimization (titles, headers, internal links)
- Marketing Lead: Review for brand voice, accuracy

**Week 4: Publishing & Promotion**
- Developer: Publish to website (CMS)
- Social Media: LinkedIn, Facebook posts
- Email: Include in weekly newsletter
- Partnerships: Share with relevant associations

### Content Guidelines

**Writing Style:**
- Swedish language (not translated from English)
- "Du" form (informal, friendly)
- Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max for readability)
- Bullet points, numbered lists (scannable)
- Concrete examples from Swedish customers

**SEO Optimization:**
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Secondary keywords in H2s
- Internal links: 3-5 per post (to product pages, other content)
- External links: 2-3 authoritative sources
- Image alt text: Descriptive, includes keywords

**Conversion Optimization:**
- CTA in first 200 words (subscribe, demo)
- Mid-post CTA (after valuable section)
- End-post CTA (primary conversion goal)
- Sidebar CTA (demo booking form)
