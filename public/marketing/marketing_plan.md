## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risks
**Risk:** Economic downturn reduces SME SaaS spending
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on ROI messaging (clear payback in 4 months)
- Offer annual plans with discounts
- Demonstrate revenue impact (more bookings from better reviews)

**Risk:** Competitors respond with lower pricing
**Mitigation:**
- Our cost structure supports 60% lower prices than incumbents
- Emphasize superior technology (Smart Routingâ„¢, AI quality)
- Lock in customers with annual contracts and integrations

### Execution Risks
**Risk:** CAC increases beyond 500 SEK as we scale
**Mitigation:**
- Diversify channels (not dependent on single channel)
- Build owned channels (SEO, partnerships) with 0 marginal CAC
- Continuously test and optimize (weekly A/B testing)

**Risk:** Marketing team hiring delays
**Mitigation:**
- Founder-led marketing initially (proven 500 SEK CAC)
- Fractional specialists before full-time hires
- Automated tools reduce headcount needs
