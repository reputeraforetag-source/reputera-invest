## Partnership Category 1: Technology Integrations

### Strategic Rationale
Swedish SMEs already use booking software, accounting tools, and CRMs. By integrating with these platforms, we become part of their existing workflow, increasing adoption and reducing churn.

### Priority Technology Partners

#### Partner 1: Bokadirekt (Booking Platform)

**Company Profile:**
- **Market Position:** Leading booking platform for Swedish SMEs
- **User Base:** 10,000+ active businesses (salons, healthcare, services)
- **Customer Overlap:** 60% of their users are target customers for Reputera

**Partnership Structure:**
- **Integration Type:** Two-way API integration
  - Reputera automatically syncs appointments from Bokadirekt
  - Sends review requests after appointment completion
  - Review data flows back to Bokadirekt dashboard

- **Go-to-Market:**
  - Listed in Bokadirekt app marketplace
  - Co-marketing: Joint webinar, case study, email to users
  - Exclusive offer: 20% off for Bokadirekt customers

- **Revenue Model:**
  - No rev share (mutually beneficial integration)
  - Co-marketing budget: 40k SEK/year

**Expected Results:**
- **Reach:** 10,000 Bokadirekt users
- **Conversion:** 2% try Reputera = 200 trials
- **Trial ‚Üí Paid:** 25% (higher due to integration) = 50 customers
- **CAC:** 40k SEK / 50 = 800 SEK
- **But:** Also reduces churn (integrated = stickier) ‚Üí lifetime value increase

**Timeline:**
- Month 1-2: Technical integration development
- Month 3: Beta testing with 10 joint customers
- Month 4: Public launch, app marketplace listing
- Month 5-6: Co-marketing campaign

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Partner Underperformance
**Mitigation:**
- Quarterly performance reviews
- 90-day check-in for new partners
- Tiered rewards (motivate performance)
- Sunset inactive partnerships

### Risk 2: Partner Conflicts
**Mitigation:**
- Clear partnership agreements (exclusivity, territories)
- Separate tiers for associations (non-competing industries)
- Transparency on partner roles

### Risk 3: Integration Complexity
**Mitigation:**
- Prioritize simple integrations first (data export/import)
- Deep API integrations only for high-value partners
- Budget 2-4 weeks per integration
- Hire integration specialist (Month 6)

### Risk 4: Commission Costs
**Mitigation:**
- Cap commissions at 30% max
- Year 1 only for most partners (not perpetual)
- Tiered commissions (lower % after Year 1)
- Focus on high LTV customers (minimize churn)
